# Grime Guardians Discord Hierarchy & Channel Guide

_Last updated: 2025-06-08_

## Recommended Category/Channel Structure

### 🛡 WELCOME
- **🟢-start-here**
  - _Purpose:_ Welcome, server intro, bot intro, navigation guide.
  - _Pin:_ "Welcome to Grime Guardians! Start here for server navigation, bot commands, and support."
- **⚠-rules-and-expectations**
  - _Purpose:_ Rules, code of conduct, escalation policy.
  - _Pin:_ "Read and acknowledge all rules. Violations may result in strikes or removal."

### 📋 ONBOARDING
- **☑-onboarding-checklist**
  - _Purpose:_ Step-by-step onboarding for new cleaners.
  - _Pin:_ "Complete all steps before your first job. Checklist auto-updates from Notion."
- **📄-submit-paperwork**
  - _Purpose:_ Upload W-9, ID, and other docs.
  - _Pin:_ "Upload required paperwork here. Contact HR for help."
- **🧪-test-clean-scheduling** _(renamed)_
  - _Purpose:_ Schedule and coordinate test cleans.
  - _Pin:_ "Post your availability for test cleans. Rhea will confirm."

### ✅ CLEANER OPERATIONS
- **☑-cleaning-checklists**
  - _Purpose:_ Access latest cleaning SOPs and checklists.
  - _Pin:_ "Always review the latest checklist before each job."
- **📸-photo-submissions**
  - _Purpose:_ Upload before/after job photos.
  - _Pin:_ "Follow photo guidelines. Nikolai will review for compliance."
- **🪧-job-board**
  - _Purpose:_ View and claim open jobs.
  - _Pin:_ "React to claim a job. Ava will confirm assignments."
- **✔-job-check-ins**
  - _Purpose:_ Post check-ins/outs. Keith/Ava log attendance and escalate here.
  - _Pin:_ "Check in 15 min before your job. Late? Ava will escalate. See escalation policy."
- **💬-cleaner-chat**
  - _Purpose:_ Peer support, Q&A, general chat.
  - _Pin:_ "Be respectful. For urgent ops, use 🚨-alerts."

### 🔥 PERFORMANCE + CULTURE
- **🗣-shoutouts**
  - _Purpose:_ Celebrate wins, MVPs, and teamwork.
  - _Pin:_ "Nominate a teammate for a shoutout! Maya posts weekly MVPs."
- **❌-strikes**
  - _Purpose:_ Log and discuss strikes/violations.
  - _Pin:_ "Strike policy: 3 strikes = review. Zara posts automated notices."

### 📚 RESOURCES
- **📑-training-materials**
  - _Purpose:_ Access training videos, SOPs, and guides.
  - _Pin:_ "All training resources are here. Ask for help if needed."

### HQ
- **🚨-alerts**
  - _Purpose:_ Urgent ops, escalations, and critical pings from Ava/Keith.
  - _Pin:_ "Ava posts escalations here (10/15 min late, critical issues). Ops team monitors this channel."

---

## Workflow Assignments
- **Check-ins, escalations, attendance:** `✔-job-check-ins` (Ava/Keith)
- **Strikes, bonuses, performance:** `❌-strikes`, `🗣-shoutouts` (Zara/Maya)
- **Urgent ops/escalations:** `🚨-alerts` (Ava)
- **Photo/SOP validation:** `📸-photo-submissions` (Emma)
- **Job board/claims:** `🪧-job-board` (future: automate job claim/assignment)
- **Onboarding:** `☑-onboarding-checklist`, `📄-submit-paperwork`, `🧪-test-clean-scheduling` (Rhea)

## Channel/Category Streamlining
- Archive or delete unused channels (e.g., `testing`).
- Use clear, action-oriented names.
- Pin a "channel purpose" message in every channel.
- Assign bots (Ava, Keith, etc.) to automate reminders, pings, and SOP links.
